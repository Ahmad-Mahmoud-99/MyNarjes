Medical Analysis Management System

In a nutshell, our project is a managerial system for medical analysis centers. It is an integrated system between the patient, the center and the accountant. As the patient will give his data to the laboratory employee and the names of the analyzes he wants to do. Then, the employee, in turn, sends a notification to the accountant so that the patient can pay the required money. Finally, the accountant sends a confirmation notification to the employee. As the employees of the center through this system will be able to enter the results of the analysis on the site without the need to fill papers. In the end the result will reach the patient electronically without the need to receive the paper file if the matter is urgent. Among the most prominent technologies used in our project: Database management system MySQL, Larave.
